Hiking!

My favourite activity in Sydney is going on all the beautiful hikes/walks surrounding the city! Even though it is a classic, one of my favourite walks is the "Bondi to Cogee". But I also enjoy going across the bridge to Bradleys Head. The best place for hiking is down south in the Royal National Park. I have done the popular 26K coastal walk that takes you from the one end to the other of the park.
